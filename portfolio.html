lask's towers of dread.

I'm the creator of lask's towers of dread. Which is a semi-popular EToH Fangame with just under 100k visits. it consists of 4 rings and 1 subrealm. It contains classic themed towers and has been formerly approved by a few well known individuals in the EToH Community.

EToH RNG

I am the founder of EToH RNG which is a game focused on collecting towers based off of luck and strategy in the style of EToH Towers. It is currently a game with roughly 200K visits however at the moment the game is at a dead state. (womp womp or sum)
